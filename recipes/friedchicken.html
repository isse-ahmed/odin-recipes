Power's Fiendishly Crispy Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl, combine buttermilk, hot sauce, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and ensure they are fully submerged in the marinade.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, up to overnight for better flavor absorption.
  4. In a separate large bowl, mix together the flour, cayenne pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Fill a deep pan with vegetable oil, about 2-3 inches deep.
  6. Remove chicken pieces one at a time from the marinade, letting excess liquid drip off.
  7. Dredge each piece in the flour mixture, making sure it's fully coated. Shake off excess flour.
  8. Carefully place the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil using tongs.
  9. Fry until golden brown and the internal temperature reaches at least 165°F (74°C), about 8-12 minutes depending on the size of the pieces.
