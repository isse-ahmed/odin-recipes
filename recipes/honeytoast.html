Denji's Super Toast

Ingredients

  • Slice of white bread
  • 1 tbsp strawberry jam
  • 1 tbsp orange jam
  • 1 tbsp plum jam
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Pinch of cinnamon

Recipe

  1. Toast bread to your liking
  2. Spread the strawberry, orange and plum jam onto the toasted bread
  3. Drizzle the honey over the toast
  4. Sprinkle the cinnamon onto the toast
  5. Add the room temperature butter to the top of the toast
