Aki's Zen Curry

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • 1.5 pounds boneless chicken, beef, or pork, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 large potato, cubed
  • 1 apple, peeled and grated
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 4 cups chicken or beef broth
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil for cooking

Recipe

  1. Melt butter in a small pan over low heat.
  2. Stir in the flour to form a roux, cooking it until it turns a light brown color.
  3. Add curry powder, garam masala, paprika, and cayenne pepper to the roux. Stir well until spices are fully incorporated. Set aside.
  4. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  6. Add the meat to the pot and cook until all sides are browned.
  7. Remove the meat and set it aside, leaving the onions in the pot.
  8. Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot.
  9. Cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  10. Return the meat to the pot.
  11. Add the grated apple, honey, soy sauce, and broth.
  12. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.
  13. Cover and simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the meat and vegetables are tender.
  14. Stir in the prepared curry base (roux) into the pot.
  15. Continue to simmer, stirring constantly, until the curry thickens to your desired consistency.
  16. Season with salt to taste.
  17. Serve hot over steamed rice.
